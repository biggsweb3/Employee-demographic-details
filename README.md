### KPIs & Metrics 
- Total Employees;This is the total number of employee
- Attriiton Rate;This is the rate at which employee leaves the company
- Average Tenure;This represents the mean of employees year at the company, current position and years since last promoted
- Average salary;This represents the mean of compensation paid to employees
